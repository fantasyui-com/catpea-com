Catpea Advanced Technological Product Emergence Architecture (CATPEA) is focused on long-term ambitious problem solving by means of well established patterns framed outside of their initial bounding principles.

The primary fundamental principle powering the Advanced Development Programs (ADP) stands as follows:

The well established shalln't be replaced by that which is unproven by the test of decades.

The primary objective for 2020-2021 is Minimum Viable Product Generation (MVPG).

Research focus for 2020-2021 is targeting MUD, Tone.js Generative Music, Generative Vector Patterns (Fashion Design), Bot Communication Frameworks, FS-Free Wiki; and underlying programming patterns and constraint solutions.
